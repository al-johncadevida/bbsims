Why Visit Us?

Barangay Bolo is one of the Barangay who have most establishments around the Municipality of Bauan. In accordance to that, people visits us for the following places: The Holy Family Parish, it is known because its big and how it can accommodate people during mass, weddings, etc. Numerous couples have made their vows and infants have been christened in the church. Another places that most talks about is thr Skye's Food Park, it is good for talks, get togethers and bonding time with friends and family. As a team, we would like to use this opportunity to introduce the Barangay and make it more accessible to people, we do not only serve the residents, the places, the datas and informations but as well as its name to continue its prosperity all throughout the more coming years.

Frequently Asked Question

How can I register?

Point the mouse cursor to the Home button and choose "Login or Signup". Choose Sign Up then provide the necessary fields with honest data then signup.

What are the benefits of using the App?

The main benefit of using the app is to make the lives around the community more favorable and adaptable to them. Easpecially that technology never ceases to stop, taking its advantages to today's world, the application will be a lot of help to the residents. Aside from people, the system also serves and makes sure that the confidentiality of all information and datas of the barangay will be highly kept. This is to certify that as the world changes, we can also adapt and learn to embrace its purpose day by day.

How can I contact the Barangay?

Point the mouse cursor to the About button and choose "Contact Us". Email us using the official email account of barangay Bolo or reach us through our Social Media Account.
